Our Story

Built Because SMBs Deserve Better IT

We saw the same problem everywhere: small and medium businesses paying $100,000+ per year for a single IT person who could not possibly cover everything. That means licensing, security, cloud, devices, helpdesk, and AI all falling on one person.

Temrink was built to change that. We offer the full scope of an enterprise IT department at a fraction of the cost. Our clients get a dedicated team of specialists instead of one overwhelmed generalist.

Today, we manage 50+ licensing products, support businesses across every province, and help companies adopt AI and automation to work smarter. From dental clinics to construction firms, we handle IT so our clients can focus on growth.
