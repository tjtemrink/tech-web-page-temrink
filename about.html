Our Story

Built for Businesses That Deserve Better IT

We started Temrink because we saw the same problem over and over. Small and medium businesses were paying too much for IT that did not work well, or they were trying to manage it all themselves and falling behind.

We believe every growing business deserves an IT team that is proactive, responsive, and actually understands how small businesses work. That is what we built.

Today, we serve businesses across Canada, managing their licenses, devices, helpdesk, and AI automation so they can focus on what they do best.
