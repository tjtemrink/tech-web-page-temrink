Our Story

Built for Businesses That Deserve Better IT

We started Temrink because we saw the same problem over and over: small and medium businesses paying too much for IT that didn't work well, or trying to manage it all themselves and falling behind.

We believe every growing business deserves an IT team that's proactive, responsive, and actually understands how SMBs work. That's what we built.

Today, we serve businesses across the GTA and British Columbia — managing their licenses, devices, helpdesk, and AI automation so they can focus on what they do best.
